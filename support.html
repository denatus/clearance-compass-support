FAQ

What is Clearance Compass?
Clearance Compass helps boaters prepare for arrivals, port formalities, and clearance planning with less time spent searching through websites, forums, and scattered articles.

How do I report a bug?
Send an email to kjeldkarim@gmail.com and include:

  • device model
  • iOS/Android version
  • app version
  • what happened
  • steps to reproduce
  • screenshots if possible

How do I report incorrect port or clearance information?
Please email the port name, country, and the information that seems incorrect or outdated.

How do I request a feature?
Feature suggestions are welcome by email.
